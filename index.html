Why moving towards Renewable Energy

Solar energy is the most abundant energy resource on earth. A Solar PV System takes advantage of the sun's energy to power homes and even businesses. As the electricity is harvested from the sun, it is both renewable and sustainable. As a type of renewable energy, generation produces no greenhouse gas emissions compared to fossil fuels. Fossil fuels create pollutants, which contribute to global warming. Whereas fossil fuels will eventually run out, the sun will always shine.
